About

This digital resume project is really just a fun, personal endeavor to showcase skills and creativity. That said, if anyone's interested, it's designed to be easily personalizable. Additionally, since it's software geared and themed, feel free to 'branch' :D out on github and make it to your taste. By editing the .po translation file, which is then compiled and integrated into the Jinja2 templates, you can easily change the layout and settings.
