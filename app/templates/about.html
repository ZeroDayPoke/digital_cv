About Me

Hello! I'm Mason, a data engineer who is deeply passionate about machine learning, neuroscience, and all things STEM. Transitioning from a diverse background in business, trade-work, and experiential marketing, I've found my true calling in software programming and machine learning. After building a strong foundation in C and Python, I'm currently focused on working through the foundational math and statistics behind neural networks while utilizing all of the machine learning tools that I can grasp. My academic journey at Oklahoma State University culminated in a degree in International Business and Chinese Studies, and included awesome experiences while studying abroad in China at Fudan University in Shanghai and the opportunity to attend the World Expo 2010 in Shanghai (I highly recommend the World Expo for your bucket list!). Although my initial goal to bridge U.S.-China relations shifted due to personal reasons, these experiences ignited my love for cultural diversity and travel. Post-graduation, my diverse roles in HVAC contracting, home renovations, and experiential marketing have taught me the power of human diversity and the importance of effective communication. These skills are now being channeled into making complex topics in computer science and machine learning more accessible. As I look toward the future, I'm thrilled at the prospect of blending my love for technology and science in a career that promises endless intellectual challenges and meaningful impact. Also, I have realized that programming, especially in the field of machine learning, is a skill that will allow me to work in any and all fields of science! Therefore, I'm fully committed to mastering machine learning and am on track to complete my curriculum at Holberton by April 2024. Beyond my professional pursuits, my interests in philosophy, cognitive science, and science fiction continue to broaden my perspective and fuel my passion for learning.
